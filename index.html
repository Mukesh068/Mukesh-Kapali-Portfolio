About

A kitchen is only as precise as its quietest section.

Chef Mukesh is a HACCP-certified Chef de Partie with over nine years in high-pressure, internationally benchmarked kitchens. His career has threaded through some of the most exacting properties in the industry — The Ritz-Carlton Jeddah, Hyatt Regency Kathmandu, The Chedi Katara in Doha, Autograph Collection NEOM, and currently The Luxury Collection, Riyadh.

He is a pre-opening specialist. Five of his seven roles have been part of launch teams — the work of writing SOPs, calibrating mise-en-place systems, training junior brigades, and holding the line on food safety from day one.
